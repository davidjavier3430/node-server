About

BIO

My name is David, I was born in 1990 in Puerto Rico. Ever since I have excelled in my studies and shown some talent at everything and anything that involves being creative. I recently completed a BFA in Design and Digital Art at the Escuela de Artes Plásticas de Puerto Rico, located in Old San Juan, P.R. I am driven by the belief that one must do things with passion or not do them at all.

Also, by having a multidisciplinary preparation and problem-solving mindset, I am passionate about creating functional and visually pleasing design that always takes into consideration the client/consumer’s needs. I find inspiration in the architecture, photography and popular culture. Also, I'm very interested in the importance of signification in the creation of art and design works.
